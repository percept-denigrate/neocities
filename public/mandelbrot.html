Mandelbrot set

The Mandelbrot set is probably the most famous matheatical fractal figure. It defined by all complex numbers c such that the sequence defined by z_0 = 0 and z_n+1 = z_n^2 + c converges.

My gf and I are painting it. We use a piece of carboard with a folding piece panel on either side. We are drawing the main figure with a blue gradient background on the main panel, and we will draw zoomed in perspectives with a magenta gradient background on the side panels.
